How It Works

Three steps, zero headaches

From setup to certificates in the time it used to take to print your sign-in sheets.

1

Register Attendees

Upload a roster, import from a form, or add attendees manually. Each person gets a unique QR code sent to their email — takes under two minutes.

2

Run Your Seminar with QR Check-In

Display your event's QR scanner on any tablet or laptop. Attendees scan in and scan out — the system tracks full attendance time automatically.

3

Certificates Auto-Sent on Checkout

The moment an attendee checks out, a branded PDF CE certificate is generated and emailed automatically. No follow-up needed — ever.

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for CE seminar organizers — not a bloated event platform you have to wrestle with.

QR Code Check-In & Check-Out

Each attendee receives a personal QR code. Staff scan arrivals and departures on any device — phone, tablet, or laptop. The system records exact times and flags anyone who leaves early, ensuring you only issue certificates for fully compliant attendance. No app download required for attendees.

Auto PDF Certificates

Professional, branded PDF CE certificates are generated instantly on checkout — pre-filled with attendee name, credit hours, course title, date, and your organization's signature.

Instant Email Delivery

Certificates are emailed the moment checkout is confirmed. Attendees receive them before they've even left the parking lot — no waiting, no chasing, no printing.

Permanent Compliance Records

Every attendance record is stored indefinitely in your dashboard. Download CSV exports, look up past attendees, and stay audit-ready at all times.

Manager Dashboard

A real-time view of every event: who's checked in, who's checked out, who still needs a certificate, and full historical reporting across all your past seminars.
